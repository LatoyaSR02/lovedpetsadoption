Our Philosophy

At Loved Pets Animal Shelter, we believe every animal deserves a loving forever home. We provide veterinary care, grooming, and screening for adoptive families to ensure the best match for every pet.

Volunteer With Us

We’re always looking for dedicated volunteers! You can help by bathing and grooming animals, socializing pets, cleaning facilities, or assisting with administrative tasks.

  1. Complete the online application.
  2. Email it to volunteer@welovepets.com.
  3. Participate in a brief phone interview.
  4. Attend our volunteer training session on the first Saturday of each month.
