Our Philosophy

At Loved Pets Animal Shelter, we believe all animals deserve a chance to find a loving forever home. We provide veterinary care and match animals with human families who will love them and keep them safe.

How to Become a Volunteer

  1. Complete the online application.
  2. Email it to volunteer@welovepets.com.
  3. Participate in a brief phone interview.
  4. Attend a volunteer training session on the first Saturday of every month.
