Our Philosophy

At Loved Pets Animal Shelter, we believe every animal deserves a second chance. We provide veterinary care, grooming, and thorough screening to ensure every pet finds the perfect forever home.

Volunteer With Us

We’re always looking for dedicated volunteers! Join our mission to care for rescued animals. Here’s how you can get involved:

  1. Complete the online application.
  2. Email it to volunteer@welovepets.com.
  3. Participate in a brief phone interview.
  4. Attend a volunteer training session held on the first Saturday of every month.
